About

I'm one of Europe's leading consumer, monetization and growth leaders, and I still love to roll up my sleeves. I spent eight years inside Spotify through its hyper-growth era, most recently as Director of Global Growth, where my team drove more than 60% of Spotify's subscriber growth. From 2025 to 2026 I was CEO of Yousician, the company behind the music apps Yousician and GuitarTuna, with 20+ million monthly active users. Through Newconic Ventures I back consumer, prosumer, AI, and subscription startups across Europe and the US.

What ties it all together is people. I understand humans across cultures, fueled by a love of other places (75+ countries and counting), and I turn that instinct into results through relentless research and A/B testing across hundreds of millions of users worldwide.

I'm AI-forward in practice, not just talk: I ship my own products, like trainwithkalle.com and trainingzones.app, and I know how to get a whole company behind an AI transformation, not just a stalled pilot. I've grown products at hundred-million-subscriber scale and from a standing start, so I know which playbook applies when.

Common questions

What do you actually do?
I come in as a fractional or interim leader for growth, product, a region, or a business unit, I run teams and get them shipping, I sit on boards, and I advise founders and leaders making hard calls. Operating-heavy or advisory-light, whichever the situation needs. The common thread is consumer monetization and growth, paired with a bias for action.
What are you best at?
Turning early consumer traction into durable, compounding revenue: lifecycle and habit, pricing, monetization and packaging, multi-channel conversion, retention cohorts, internationalization. I learned it at hundred-million-subscriber scale at Spotify, and I've applied it from a standing start ever since.
Who do you work with?
Consumer and prosumer companies tackling growth challenges on more than one front: product, monetization, user acquisition, culture and leadership. Companies going through an AI transformation. From pre-seed founders to seasoned leadership teams. If your growth depends on people choosing to pay you again and again, that's my world.
Are you open to a full-time role?
Yes, for the right company. I'm selective, but the right mission, team, and mandate would win, and I'd go all in. The rest of the time I run a few fractional, board, and advisory engagements at once. Either way, book a call and make the case.
What is Newconic Ventures?
My angel investing and advisory vehicle, running since 2019. I back pre-seed and seed consumer, prosumer, AI, and subscription startups across the EU and US, 18+ to date. Two exits so far: Butter to Miro, and Petli to Agria. Advisory work includes Peloton, Knowunity, and Kittl.
Where are you based?
Berlin. Native German, fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, and I've worked across Berlin, Stockholm, New York, and Helsinki, plus pretty much every major city in the world. I travel a lot; 75 countries so far.
How do we start?
Book a call. Bring the hard question; I'll bring an honest answer and a view on what to do next.
