About

Sven Ahrens is one of Europe's leading consumer subscription and growth experts. He spent eight years at Spotify during the hyper-growth era, most recently as Director of Global Growth, where his team drove 60+% of Spotify's subscriber growth. From 2025 to 2026 he was CEO at Yousician, the music app developer behind Yousician and GuitarTuna with 20+ million monthly active users worldwide. He is the founder of Newconic Ventures, his angel investing and advisory vehicle backing consumer, AI and subscription companies across Europe and the US.
